Dashboard Corporate Banking - Bci Bank

Mar 15, 2022

Role

UX/Product Owner

Client

BCI Bank is one of Chile's leading banks, with operations in Peru and the United States. It became the largest bank in Florida after acquiring City National Bank.

Challenge

BCI is a major player in corporate banking, with "360 Connect" being the private platform for companies to manage their banking needs. As part of continuous improvement, we aimed to enhance the user experience upon logging in, to add more functionalities, improve information hierarchy, and enhance accessibility.

Research and Discovery

With a product like 360 Connect, which had over 280k monthly users, we had ample data to analyze user behavior via Google Analytics and conduct direct interviews for qualitative insights on the current dashboard. We also researched other dashboards across various platforms.

Ideation

With all the information gathered during research, we prioritized new dashboard features such as consolidated multi-currency balances, dynamic shortcuts based on user, summary of pending operations, and access to the help center. These were designed in modular blocks that clients could add or remove as they preferred. Additionally, we integrated a new design system that allowed for the inclusion of graphs, enhancing visual language and enabling quicker reading.

New Dashboard.

Testing

We conducted various testing stages, starting with usability tests on wireframes, followed by high-fidelity prototypes. In the final stage, a beta version was launched for a select group of clients to evaluate their response to the new solution. We were very satisfied as the behavior met our expectations.

Usability Testing.

Results

In the first 3 months, we achieved:

  • A 122% increase in user interaction with the new dashboard.
  • A 73% reduction in access to the "balances" module, now directly viewable on the dashboard.
  • A CSAT of 4.9/5.
